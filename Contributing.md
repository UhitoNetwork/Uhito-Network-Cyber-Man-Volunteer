# Contributing to the Cyber-Man Program

We welcome:
- Suggestions for new templates or guides
- Feedback from your volunteer experience
- Ideas for improving the pathway

We do NOT accept:
- Pull requests that copy our content elsewhere
- Requests to remove attribution
- Commercial collaboration offers

## How to Contribute
1. Open an Issue with your idea
2. Tell us about your experience as a volunteer
3. If you've created useful materials you'd like to share, we may add them (with credit)
