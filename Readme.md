# Cyber-Man Volunteer Program

This repository is for **Cyber-Men** – people who want to use their cybersecurity knowledge to protect their communities, document their work, and build a portfolio that leads to jobs.

---

### What You'll Find Here

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `cyberman-program/` | Who we are, why we volunteer, our responsibilities and ethics |
| `before-you-start/` | Critical legal warnings – read this first |
| `pathway/` | Step‑by‑step guide from learning to community service |
| `content-creation/` | How to create awareness materials, with examples |
| `documentation/` | Why and how to document your work (with templates) |
| `portfolio/` | How to use your volunteer work on LinkedIn and job applications |
| `community/` | Connect with other Cyber-Men, share your work, get feedback |

---

### Your Journey as a Cyber-Man

1. **Read everything in `before-you-start/`** – understand the legal risks.
2. **Follow the `pathway/`** – learn, help privately, get certified, then serve your community.
3. **Use `content-creation/`** to make simple, effective awareness materials.
4. **Document your work** using the templates in `documentation/`.
5. **Build your portfolio** with guidance from `portfolio/`.
6. **Share and connect** in `community/`.

---

### Why Volunteer?

- Discover your real abilities
- Build skills that employers look for
- Show ethics and responsibility
- Increase your chance of getting hired
- Help people in your area stay safe
