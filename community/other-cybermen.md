# Connecting with Other Cyber-Men

You are not alone. There are other volunteers like you.

---

### How to Connect

- **Email us** at uhitonetwork@gmail.com and ask to be connected with volunteers in your area or field.
- **Follow #CyberMan** on LinkedIn and social media to see what others are doing.
- **Share your work** (see `share-your-work.md`) and invite others to comment.

---

### Why Connect
- Share ideas and materials
- Learn from each other's experiences
- Support each other when facing challenges
- Build a network that can lead to job opportunities

---

### Be Respectful

When connecting with other volunteers:
- Respect their time and privacy
- Give credit where it's due
- Offer help if you can

---

**Together, we can build a community of protectors.**
