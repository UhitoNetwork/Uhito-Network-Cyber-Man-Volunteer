# Share Your Work with the Community

You've done amazing things. Let others learn from you.

---

### How to Share

1. **Write an impact story** (use the template in `documentation/templates/impact-story.md`).
2. **Anonymise it** – remove real names and personal details.
3. **Send it to us** at uhitonetwork@gmail.com.
4. **We may feature it** (with your permission) in our success stories or on social media.

---

### What We're Looking For
- Real examples of helping people
- Challenges you faced
- Lessons learned
- Creative materials you created

---

### Why Share?
- Inspire other Cyber-Men
- Build your reputation
- Show employers you're part of a community
- Get feedback and recognition

---

**Your story could be the one that encourages someone else to start.**
