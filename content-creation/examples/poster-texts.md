# Example Poster Texts

## Poster 1: Stop and Think
**Big title:** STOP  
**Subtitle:** Before you click, ask yourself:  
- Do I know this sender?  
- Is this too good to be true?  
- Am I being rushed?  

**Footer:** When in doubt, don't click. Talk to someone you trust.

---

## Poster 2: Protect Your Accounts
**Big title:** 3 STEPS TO SAFETY  
1. Use a different password for every account  
2. Turn on two‑step verification (2FA)  
3. Update your phone and computer  

**Footer:** It takes 10 minutes. It saves years of headache.

---

## Poster 3: Watch Out for Scams
**Big title:** SCAM ALERT  
**Common tricks:**  
- Fake bank calls  
- Gift card requests  
- "You won a prize" messages  
- Urgent emails from "boss" or "family"  

**Footer:** If it feels wrong, it probably is. Hang up and check.

---

## Poster 4: Public Wi‑Fi Warning
**Big title:** FREE WI‑FI? BE CAREFUL.  
- Others can see what you do  
- Avoid banking and email  
- Use a VPN if you must connect  

**Footer:** Your data is worth more than free internet.

---
