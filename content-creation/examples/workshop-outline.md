# Example Workshop Outline (60 Minutes)

## Before You Start
- Read the `cyberman-program/` folder.
- Choose 2–3 topics from the Awareness Repository warnings/guides.
- Print some quick‑tips checklists to hand out.

---

## Workshop Outline

### 0–5 min: Welcome
- Introduce yourself (your name, that you're a Cyber-Man volunteer)
- "I'm here to share simple ways to stay safe online – no technical talk."

### 5–15 min: One Story
- Pick one story from the Awareness Repository (e.g., "The Bank Call")
- Read it out loud or tell it in your own words
- Ask: "Has anyone heard of something like this?"

### 15–30 min: Two Warnings
- Choose two warnings (e.g., phishing SMS and fake tech support)
- Explain each in simple terms
- Show examples if you have them (screenshots of scam messages)

### 30–45 min: Simple Actions
- Walk through one guide, e.g., "Protect Your Accounts"
- Show them how to turn on 2FA on their phones (if they're comfortable)
- Hand out the printable checklist

### 45–55 min: Questions & Sharing
- Let people ask questions
- Encourage them to share their own experiences (if comfortable)

### 55–60 min: Wrap Up
- Remind them: "You can be a Cyber-Man too – start by protecting yourself, then help family."
- Give them the Awareness Repository link
- Thank them for coming
