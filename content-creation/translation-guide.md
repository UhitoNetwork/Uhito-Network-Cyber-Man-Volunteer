# Translation Guide – Making Content for Your Community

If your community speaks a different language, you can translate our materials.

### What to Translate
- Warnings (SIM swap, fake tech support, etc.)
- Quick‑tips checklists
- Simple guides

### How to Translate
1. Copy the original text.
2. Translate it into your language – keep the meaning, not word‑for‑word.
3. Use simple words that everyone in your community understands.
4. Add a note: "Translated from Uhito Network materials" with our email.

### What NOT to Change
- Don't remove the copyright notice.
- Don't add your own technical advice unless you're certified.
- Don't change the core message.

### Share Your Translation
Email us at uhitonetwork@gmail.com with your translated files. We may add them to a community translations folder (with credit to you).
