# Who Is a Cyber-Man Volunteer?

A **Cyber-Man Volunteer** is someone who:
- Has learned cybersecurity basics (or is learning)
- Wants to help their community stay safe online
- Takes full responsibility for what they share and do
- Documents their work to show skills and ethics
- Uses volunteering as a path to employment

### They Are:
- Learners and helpers
- Responsible for their own actions
- Careful not to cross legal lines
- Building proof of their abilities

### They Are NOT:
- Hackers or vigilantes
- Giving advice beyond their certification
- Sharing dangerous tools or methods
- Working without documentation
