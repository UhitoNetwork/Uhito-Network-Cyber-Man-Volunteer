# What to Document

### Document These Things

✅ **What you did** – "Helped my mother set up 2FA on her email and Facebook."

✅ **Who you helped** – Use general terms: "elderly neighbour," "local shop owner" – never real names without permission.

✅ **When you did it** – Date and time.

✅ **What you used** – Which guide or warning you shared.

✅ **What happened afterwards** – "She received a scam text later and recognised it because of our talk."

✅ **What you learned** – "I realised I need to study more about SIM swap attacks."

✅ **Challenges** – "He was confused about the difference between password and 2FA."
