# Step 3: Get Certified

To serve the public as a Cyber-Man, you need proper certification.

### Why Certification Matters
- It proves you know what you're talking about.
- It protects you legally – you can say "I'm certified in this area."
- It builds trust with the community.
- Employers look for it.

### Recommended Certifications
- **Entry Level:** Google Cybersecurity Certificate, CompTIA Security+
- **Intermediate:** CompTIA CySA+, Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)
- **Specialised:** Cisco certifications, cloud security certs

### How to Get Certified Without Much Money
- Use financial aid on Coursera for Google certificates.
- Study free materials (Professor Messer, YouTube) and pay only for the exam.
- Some platforms (like Cisco) offer free certificates upon completion.

### While You Study
Keep helping privately. Keep documenting. Keep learning.
