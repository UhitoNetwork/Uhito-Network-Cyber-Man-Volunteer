# How to Use Your Volunteer Work on LinkedIn

Your volunteer work is **proof** of your skills. Here's how to show it on LinkedIn.

---

### Add a Volunteer Section

1. Go to your LinkedIn profile.
2. Click "Add profile section" → "Volunteer experience" → "Add volunteer experience."
3. Fill in:
   - **Organization:** Uhito Network – Cyber-Man Program
   - **Role:** Cyber-Man Volunteer
   - **Description:** Briefly describe what you do (e.g., "Create simple awareness materials to help my community avoid online scams.")
   - **Date started:** [when you began]
   - **Date ended:** (leave blank if ongoing)

---

### Write Posts About Your Work

Share your impact stories (anonymised). Example post:

> "Today I helped a neighbour recognise a fake tech support call. She was about to give remote access to her computer. We talked through the signs – urgency, strange caller ID, request for payment by gift card. She hung up and reported the number.  
> Small actions like this build safer communities. #CyberMan #Volunteer #CyberSecurity"

---

### Add Volunteer Work to Your Experience

You can also mention it in your regular experience section if it's relevant.

---

### Connect with Other Cyber-Men

Follow and connect with people who share similar values. Engage with their posts.

---

**Your volunteer work shows ethics, communication, and real-world impact – things employers love.**
